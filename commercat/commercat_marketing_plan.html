01 — Strategy

Organic first, always.

The dupe finder is inherently viral. One good screen recording acquiring a user at $0 is worth more than a $15 paid click. Every paid dollar follows proof of organic conversion — never precedes it.

$0 CAC
The dupe finder is the marketing
When a user finds a $400 jacket for $22 and shares it on Stories, that content acquires 10–50 new users at zero cost. The product is the ad. Every piece of paid marketing exists only to amplify what organic already proves is working.

Strategic principles
PRINCIPLE 01
No paid until organic converts
Paid ads launch in month 3 only — and only for retargeting visitors who already found us organically. Cold prospecting doesn't start until month 4, and only if organic CVR data supports it.
PRINCIPLE 02
Nano before macro
Start with 3K–20K follower creators. They're cheaper, more trusted, and their audiences are more engaged. Macro influencers (200K+) don't appear until year 2 — only after nano/micro is proven and ROI-positive.
PRINCIPLE 03
Referral compounds forever
Wallet credit referrals ($5 give / $5 get) activate month 3. Every referred user who orders becomes another referrer. Unlike paid ads, this channel gets cheaper per user over time, not more expensive.

02 — Phases

Three gears, not one.

Each phase has a single primary focus. Spark builds the content machine. Scale adds paid amplification. Compound lets the flywheel run.

Phase 1
Spark
Months 1–3
Prove the dupe finder concept. Build organic traction before spending a dollar on paid ads. Content machine is established, influencer roster seeded, first referrals in motion.

07 — Calendar

24 months, week by week.

Key milestones, channel activations, and decision points across the full two-year plan.

Month 1
Launch day
Announcement to existing community. Daily dupe finder content begins. Facebook shopping groups seeded. Target: 250 active users on day 1.
Month 2
First influencer campaign
3 nano-influencers briefed (5K–15K followers). Dupe finder format. Gifted orders + $50–150 fee each. PR outreach to shopping press begins.
Month 3
Paid ads launch + referral program
First $700 on TikTok — retargeting only. Referral live: $5 wallet credit for referrer, $5 discount for new user. Influencer roster grows to 5.
Month 4–5
Paid social scale-up
Meta ads launched alongside TikTok. Lookalike audiences built from first 500 signups. Influencer roster: 8 active creators. First micro-influencer test.
Month 6
1,200 MAU — mid-year review
Channel efficiency review. Kill lowest-performing channels. BOG Pay rate negotiation begins. TikTok Shop / affiliate test starts.
Month 9
VAT threshold + mid-tier influencer test
2,100 MAU. Commission crosses 100K GEL — VAT registration. Marketing budget increases to $3,000/mo. First mid-tier influencer (60K–200K) test.
Month 12
Year 1 close — subscription preview
3,200 MAU. Pre-announcement of subscription tiers. Waitlist mechanic for early adopters. $59,654 cumulative net profit in bank.
Month 13 — KEY MOMENT
Monetisation flip
Subscriptions go live. Every user who hits their daily search limit gets a one-time 30-day free Pro trial offer in chat. Revenue nearly triples overnight. Forwarder cashback activates.
Month 15–18
Organic flywheel in full effect
4,600–6,200 MAU. Paid % of spend falls as referral and organic compound. Marketing team expands to 1 dedicated hire. First macro-influencer (200K+) collaboration.
Month 21
New market evaluation
8,000 MAU. Review: which countries show organic signups with zero paid spend? If one market clears 5% of total signups → localise and launch. First real geo-expansion decision.
Month 22–24 — KEY MOMENT
Peak season push
Holiday shopping, 11.11, Black Friday campaigns. Heaviest paid investment of Y2. Target 10,000 MAU by month 24. $91,982 net profit per month at end.
