00 — Foundation

What Commercat is

One sentence: a ChatGPT-style personal shopper that finds and buys anything from Chinese marketplaces, with transparent GEL pricing and delivery to your freight forwarder's warehouse.

The brand in three words

Commercat is smart, direct, and accessible. Not corporate, not playful, not premium — just a genuinely useful tool that respects your time and your money.

Brand Pillars
🎯
Radical transparency
Every price is explained. Item cost. FX buffer. Service fee. No hidden charges, ever. The two-line price display isn't a legal requirement — it's who we are.
⚡
Speed as a feature
Under two seconds from query to results. The product is fast by design — not just technically, but in how we communicate. Short sentences. Direct answers.
🇬🇪
Built for global shoppers
A global product that sources from China for shoppers anywhere. Local currency-first. Local language first. The buying experience is global — the sourcing is Chinese.

04 — Voice & Tone

How we speak

Commercat talks like a knowledgeable friend, not a customer service bot. Direct, warm, globally accessible. Never corporate, never overly casual.

Personality traits
🧭
Direct
Get to the point immediately. Lead with what matters. No preamble, no filler.
"Found it. 4 options under your budget. Closest match: 89% similar."
🤝
Warm
Helpful without being sycophantic. Knowledgeable without being condescending.
"That size usually runs small on this brand — I'd go one up."
🎯
Precise
Use exact numbers. Specific details. Never vague. "Around 70 GEL" becomes "67.93 GEL".
"Item cost 61.75 GEL · Fee 6.18 GEL · Total 67.93 GEL."
🇬🇪
Local-first
Write in the user's language first. Honour local shopping culture and sensibility. The product adapts to each market — it doesn't impose one.
"Price: $18.40. Ships to your forwarder in 3–7 days."
🔍
Honest
If something might be a dupe, say so. If sizing is uncertain, say so. Trust over spin.
"Looks like a Zara dupe — might not be exact materials, but close."
⚡
Efficient
Short messages. No redundancy. Users are here to shop, not read paragraphs.
"Ordered. You'll hear from me when it ships."
Do say / don't say
Say this
Found 5 options. Here's the closest match — 89% similar to your photo, 67.93 GEL.
Not this
Great news! I've successfully located multiple products that may potentially match your requirements!
Say this
Payment confirmed. Purchasing your item now — usually takes 24 hours.
Not this
Your payment has been successfully processed and we will endeavour to purchase your item as soon as possible!
Say this
This looks like a designer dupe — quality may differ from the original.
Not this
Please note that this product may be an inspired-by design similar to luxury items.
Say this
That seller's sizing runs small. Order one size up.
Not this
Based on our analysis of the available sizing information, it appears the sizing may vary.

06 — Motion

Animation principles

Motion should feel fast and purposeful — never decorative. Transitions exist to confirm actions and orient users, not to entertain.

Timing values
TokenDurationEasingUse
instant80mslinearButton press states, immediate feedback
fast150msease-outHover states, tooltip appear, toggle
base200msease-outModal open, dropdown, tab switch
slow300msease-in-outSlide-in panels, basket open
crawl500msease-in-outPage transitions (use sparingly)
Specific animations
InteractionAnimationNotes
Chat message appearsFade + slide 6px up, 150ms ease-outEach message animates independently as it arrives
Streaming textCharacters appear left-to-right, no animationThe typing itself is the animation — don't add more
Product cards loadFade in, staggered 30ms between cardsMax 5 cards visible, rest reveal on scroll
Basket openSlide in from right (desktop), bottom sheet up (mobile), 300msBackground dims to rgba(0,0,0,0.3)
Button pressscale(0.98), 80ms linearSubtle — just enough to confirm the tap
Order status updatePulse green circle once, 600msStatus badge pulses once, then settles
Search limit promptSlide in as a chat bubble, same as AI messageNo jarring modal — gentle in-chat prompt
Motion don'ts
🚫
No loading spinners
Search takes under 2 seconds. Use a simple animated ellipsis in the chat bubble ("...") instead of a spinner.
🚫
No bounce or spring
Ease-out only for UI elements. Spring physics add playfulness we don't want — Commercat is a tool.
🚫
No decorative animation
Animated backgrounds, floating particles, lottie illustrations — none of it. Motion is for feedback only.
07 — Social & Marketing

Content guidelines

Commercat's content is screen recordings, haul reveals, and price comparisons. Never polished ad campaigns. The product should look real because it is real.

Photography & video style
Screen recording Raw Commercat session
Natural light Haul reveal, real items
Before / after Price comparison
📱
Screen first
The most compelling content is unedited screen recordings of the dupe finder in action. The price reveal IS the content.
🌞
Natural settings
Film hauls at home, in natural light. No studio, no props, no artificial styling. Authenticity is the brand.
🇬🇪
Local language by default
All social copy in the local language of the market. Hashtags in local language. The content should feel native to wherever it's posted.
Instagram / TikTok caption rules
RuleExample
Lead with the price reveal"450 GEL Zara jacket → 22 GEL on Commercat 🐱"
Short sentences, line breaksNot one long paragraph. Break after every 1–2 sentences.
Emoji sparingly — max 2–3 per postThe cat emoji (🐱) is our signature. Use it at the end of price reveals.
Always include the link or @commercat_geEvery post drives to the platform
HashtagsUse local-language hashtags in each market. Include #commercat in every post.
The cat emoji
🐱
Our signature moment

The 🐱 emoji appears at the end of every price reveal — in captions, in chat responses to successful finds, and in push notifications for price drops. It's the "Commercat" punchline. Don't overuse it. One per post maximum.
